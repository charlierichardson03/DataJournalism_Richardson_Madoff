Analysis

The information industry, like many other industries, was affected heavily by the housing crisis and the dot-com crash. When the dot-com bubble burst in 2001, employment dropped 14%. The industry recovered until the housing crisis, when it dropped 8%. During the COVID-19 pandemic, the unemployment rate in the finance industry was hit hard and did not really recover, dropping another 6%.
