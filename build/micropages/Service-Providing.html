Analysis

Although the service industry follows the same general trends as many other industries, it has been steadily strengthening for about 20 years. The crisis in 2008-9 only briefly halted the industry’s climb and it was not at all affected by the dot com crash in 2001. Between 1998 and 2020, the employment numbers increased 36%. The industry was hit hard during the pandemic and is now only at 88% of what it once was.
