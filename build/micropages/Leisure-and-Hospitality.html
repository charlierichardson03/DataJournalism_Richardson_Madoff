Analysis

Similar to many other industries, Leisure and Hospitality has been steadily growing since 1998, almost doubling its size in the past two decades. As one could expect due to the lack of travel to New York over the past year, Covid-19 severely impacted the Leisure and Hospitality industry. The number of employed people went from 41,540 to 14,800, a 64% decrease. It has also been slow to recover, as travel has not significantly rebounded.
