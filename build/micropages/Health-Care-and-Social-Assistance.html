Analysis

Health Care and Social Assistance has been growing since 1998 and has been mostly unaffected by crises, besides Covid-19. Despite Health Care’s importance in solving the Covid-19 crisis, employment numbers dropped from 82,230 to 72,410 just from March to April. Although this is a much less significant drop than in some other industries, such as Leisure and Hospitality, it is still surprising that the number of people employed in healthcare decreased during a pandemic.
