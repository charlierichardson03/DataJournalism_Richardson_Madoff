Analysis

The number of people employed in Education has consistently increased since 1998. As expected, every year following May, there is a decrease in the number of people employed in Educational Services. For example, in 2001 from May to June, employment dropped from 15,580 to 13,230. However, every year by September, the number of employed people returns to its value prior to Summer.
