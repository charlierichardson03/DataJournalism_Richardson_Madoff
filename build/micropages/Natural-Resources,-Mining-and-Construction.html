Analysis

The Natural Resources, Mining, and Construction industry follows the same patterns as many other industries do. It is interesting to note though that the industry was affected more by the dot com crash in the early 2000s than it was during the COVID-19 pandemic. During the early 2000s, nearly 10% of industry workers lost their jobs while around 8% lost their jobs during the first few months of COVID-19.
