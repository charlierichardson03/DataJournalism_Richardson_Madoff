Analysis

The number of people employed in the Accommodation and Food Services industry has doubled since 1998. The industry was not affected severely by the financial crisis, but its employment numbers dropped from 32,920 to 11,390 following the pandemic. This demonstrates the extent to which Covid-19 impacted the Accommodation and Food Services industry.
