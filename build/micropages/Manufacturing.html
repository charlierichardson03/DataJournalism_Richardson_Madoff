Analysis

Manufacturing has been on a heavy decline since 1998. The decline was accelerated by the economic crisis in 2008 and in 2000 like most industries. Now, the industry is at 34% employment compared to what it was during 1998. During the first few months of COVID-19, the industry dropped to about 80% of what it was beforehand.
