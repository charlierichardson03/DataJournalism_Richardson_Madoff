Analysis

The Trade, Transportation, and Utilities industries follow the same trends as many other industries, peaking in early 2000, and in 2019. The industry seemed to have large problems during 2003 and 2004, which could have been caused by some sort of legislation that made it much harder to trade out of New York City. The industry was hit much harder during the early 2000s, however, with employment dropping 7% between 2001 and 2003 compared to 4% between 2008 and 2009. During the first few months of COVID-19, the industry experienced an 11% drop in employment.
