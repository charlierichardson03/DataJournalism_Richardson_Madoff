Analysis

Other Services is one of the least volatile industries. Throughout the past several decades, it has been steadily increasing in employment numbers, and it was not affected that severely by any crises with the exception of Covid-19. From March to April, employment dropped from 19,490 to 13,710, and even now, employment numbers remain 30 thousand lower than they were prior to the pandemic.
