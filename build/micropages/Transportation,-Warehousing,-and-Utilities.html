Analysis

The Transportation, Warehousing, and Utilities industry has suffered a lot over the years, following the same trends as many other industries. The employment numbers dropped a lot during the dot com crash and during the housing crisis but steadily climbed afterwards until the COVID-19 pandemic. From the end of the housing crisis until 2019, the industry recovered nearly 24%. During the COVID-19 pandemic, the numbers dropped about 8%.
