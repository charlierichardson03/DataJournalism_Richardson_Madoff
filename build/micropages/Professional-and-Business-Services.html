Analysis

Although the Professional and Business Services industry was hit during the dot com crash and the housing crisis, it has overall grown about 50% between 1998 and 2020. Between 2010 and 2019, employment grew 40%. During the pandemic, however, the industry suffered, dropping nearly 12%.
