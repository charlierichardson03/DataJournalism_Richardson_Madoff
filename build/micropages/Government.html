Analysis

Throughout the past several decades within government, the number of employed people has been slowly rising. However, around the start of Summer, employment numbers usually drop, especially in the late 1990’s and early 2000’s. During the 2008 Financial Crisis, Government employment numbers were not affected that severely. The Government industry was hit hard by Covid-19, as employment numbers dropped from 59,590 to 51,190 from March to June. However, relative to other industries, it had a fairly quick recovery. By August, employment numbers were matching those at the start of the pandemic.
